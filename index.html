About Me

Artist Portrait

Welcome to my artistic world. I'm Jessie, a passionate artist specializing in live event painting and luxury brand illustration. With over a decade of experience, I've perfected the art of capturing fleeting moments and transforming them into lasting memories.

My journey began at the prestigious Art Institute, where I honed my skills in classical oil painting techniques. This foundation allows me to create artworks that blend traditional elegance with contemporary vision.

Specializing in weddings and high-profile events, I work discreetly to capture the essence of your special day. Each painting is created on premium linen canvas using only the finest professional-grade oils, resulting in museum-quality heirlooms that will be cherished for generations.

For luxury brands, I create bespoke illustrations that elevate visual identities through fine art. From packaging to limited edition artworks, my pieces tell compelling brand stories through brushstrokes.
